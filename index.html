Why InfiniMind

Child Safety First

Built for Parents Who Are Rightly Careful

We built InfiniMind for the parent who isn't anti-technology — just unconvinced that "AI academy" and "eight-year-old" belong together without real safeguards. So we made the safeguards the product.

👥

Always in a Group

Never unsupervised one-on-one sessions between a child and a mentor. Every class runs live in a small group setting.

✅

Verified Mentors Only

Every mentor passes a background check and completes specialized training for teaching young learners before their first class.

👀

Open-Door Policy

Sit in on any class at any time. No separate parent account, no scheduling needed — your child's classroom is always open to you.

🔐

Minimal Data Collection

We collect only what's needed to run a class: a name, grade, and parent contact. We never build advertising profiles on children.

🚫

No Long-Term Lock-in

Month-to-month only. Pause or cancel anytime — we'd rather you stay because it's working, not because you're trapped.

🛡️

Your Child's Safety Is Non-Negotiable

Our child protection framework is reviewed annually. Every safeguard exists because a parent deserves to feel completely comfortable from day one.
